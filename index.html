Game Overview

Solo Leveling: ARISE is an action-RPG game created and released by Netmarble that is designed for mobile devices with compatibility for PCs. It is inspired by the incredibly famous Solo Leveling manhwa that was adapted into an anime. The story revolves around Sung Jinwoo, the weakest hunter of humanity, as he sets off on a mission that ends up changing both his world and the entire planet due to shocking discoveries made during his journey. It is an amazing tale with numerous unexpected emotional moments, blending a frenzied action style with memorable fights and powerful moments.

Tackle Ever-evolving Gameplay

Play as Sung Jinwoo, the main character from the webtoon. Level up to learn new skills! Experience beautiful, action-filled gameplay and dynamic combat through various skill and weapon combinations!

Join Forces with Other Hunters

Take on fast-paced gameplay by teaming up with hunters from the original webtoon. Master each hunter's abilities and form strategies to combine their powers to quickly clear each gate!

Challenge Gates and Boss Raids

Magic beasts from the original webtoon are waiting to challenge you inside every gate. Form strategies using various skills, hunters, and tactics to defeat them!
