Characters

Sung Jinwoo

A special hunter who can "level up". After the Double Dungeon Incident, this former E-Rank hunter gained access to a "system" that helped him become more powerful. Though once nicknamed The Weakest Hunter of All Mankind, he now has the ability to turn the dead into his Shadow Soldiers.

Lee Joohee

A timid B-Rank Healer and a member of the Hunters Association. She had no intention of becoming a hunter, but gave in to a fellow staff member's persistent badgering. She does hunting work as a volunteer, so she only participates in low-difficulty raids to help the weaker hunters.

Yoo Jinho

The second son of Yoo Myunghan, the president of Yoojin Construction. Jinho befriended Sung Jinwoo through a twist of fate and several raids. He is one of the few people that Sung Jinwoo fully trusts.

Cha Hae In

The 9th S-Rank hunter discovered in South Korea and the vice guild master of the Hunters Guild. She was the strongest hunter in South Korea before Sung Jinwoo made his debut. Due to her special ability to smell mana, she goes about life with a handkerchief covering her nose. She is fascinated to discover that Sung Jinwoo is the only hunter she's ever encountered with a pleasant scent.

Kim Chul

An A-Rank Tank and a rising star in the White Tiger Guild. He has great confidence in his abilities, but he's simple, ignorant, and superficial.

Emma Laurent

A kind-hearted hero who's always willing to step up when someone needs help. She has a bright and cheerful personality and plays the role of mood maker. She's investigating the "Raid Break" incident with Alicia Blanche.

Choi Jong-In

The guild master of the Hunters Guild, the best Guild in South Korea. He's been nicknamed "The Ultimate Hunter." This S-Rank Mage-type hunter mainly uses his Fire Magic, which can burn down a building faster than the blink of an eye.

Woo Jinchul

The Chief Inspector of the Hunters Association Surveillance Team. He's an A-Rank, but he is nearly on par with an S-Rank. He joined the Hunters Association with the desire to become a hunter who protects fellow hunters. As Chairman Go's assistant, he frequently crosses paths with Sung Jinwoo.

Kang Taeshik

A surveillance agent with the Korean Hunters Association Surveillance Team. Unexpected "accidents" always happen when he's dispatched, and the criminals he monitors often get caught in these "accidents" and die.

Baek Yoonho

The guild master of the White Tiger Guild and an S-Rank hunter. Originally, he was one of the four founding members of the Fiend Guild. After parting ways with them, he took guild members from his previous Guild to found the White Tigers. He has an ability called "Beast Transformation," which allows him to transform into a frightening Magic Beast.

Lim Tae-Gyu

The guild master and founder of Korea's very first guild, the Fiend Guild. Though the guild was once extremely reputable, its status deteriorated when Baek Yoonho separated from them and gained power, at which point the Hunters overtook the guild. His main weapons are Magic Arrows, made of concentrated magical energy.

Hwang Dongsoo

Korea's 8th S-Rank Hunter. He left Korea and joined the Scavenger Guild in the United States. He has a lot of pride in his powers. He is the brother of Hwang Dongsuk, a C-Rank hunter.

Hwang Dongsuk

A guildless C-Rank Tank who leads a private Strike Squad. He kills people for his own benefit. After all, "What happens in the dungeons stays in the dungeons."

Kim Sangshik

A D-Rank hunter who uses swordsmanship to slay magic beasts. Because he values his family above all else, he focuses on just making it home at the end of the day, living by the rule "Prioritize survival over earnings".

Song Chiyul

A C-Rank mage-type hunter. He's a skilled kendo practitioner, but rarely uses a sword in the dungeons due to the nature of his magical abilities. After losing his family in an accident caused by the Hunters Association's carelessness, he's become a volunteer hunter, clearing every dungeon he's assigned without fail.
